Posted on August 24, 2013 at 9:00 PM

123DCatch is a powerful tool to create a 3D scans of artifacts. The app can be downloaded on phones or tablets for free. It is also very straightforward to use as the app guides you through the process. Once the app as been opened select create a new catch. To create the scan it is necessary to take multiple photos from a vareity of angles and heights. The key however is to ensure that the object itself remains in a single location.
