About In My Grandfather's Basement

In Your Grandfather’s Basement is a crowdsourcing website that enables contributors to share and preserve their own heritage on their own terms. I designed the site in honour of my Grandfather. My Grandfather and I were always very close. As a kid, I would love listening to his stories and looking at the things his father and grandfather had given to him. Today I cherish the things that he passed on to me. In Your Grandfather’s Basement is a site dedicated to preserving, sharing and learning about our personal heritage and history.

The website allows for individuals to store and preserve a digital copy of family heirlooms, photos, and information. These digital artifacts can then be shared with friends, family or used within larger crowdsourced research projects or digital exhibitions. These latter categories represent the learn goal. In Your Grandfather’s Basement is at its core a crowdsourcing experiment. The various member uploads will form a database of heritage material previously inaccessible. With this material, website members and researchers can create crowdsourced projects and digital exhibitions to expand our knowledge and understanding on a variety of topics.
